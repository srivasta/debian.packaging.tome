#####R=== Corrupted Race ===

#####GDescription
Along the way, some creatures are exposed to the dark powers of Morgoth and 
become corrupted by their experience. People who have felt these forces will find
that their abilities and physical appearance will change randomly as they learn
and grow.

#####GStat Modifiers
Strength       0
Intelligence   0
Wisdom         0  
Dexterity      0   
Constitution   0   
Charisma       0      
Hit Dice       0 sides
Exp Penalty    +20%

Instead of bonuses or penalties to stats, those who have been corrupted gain or 
lose corruptions each time they gain a level.

#####GStarting Equipment
A corrupted character begins the game with:
    Some rations
    Some torches
